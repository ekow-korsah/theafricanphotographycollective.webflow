PROJECTS

Our projects are designed to be the fields on which we play to attain our goals. The successes of these projects, both in the past and in the future have and will always serve as a catalyst for The African Photography to do more.

“ The Industry that built itself”

This is a documentary about the strength, struggles and independence of the African creative, specifically the Ugandan Photographer/Filmmaker ( creative ).

This Documentary filmed in Uganda, by Ugandans, highlights the potential, strength and self starting spirit of the Ugandan Filmmaking and Photography Community and it’s ability to break boundaries and change negative narratives of the African Filmmaker and Photographer.

Featured personalities -Usama Mukwaya, Rhonnie Nkalubo

Connect and Create

This is an African Photography Collective project that’s designed as a conversation to allow individuals within our community pass on necessary information, share ideas and experiences, connect and inspire each other. The Collective Class

The African Photography Collective designed this workshop to empower and enlighten the Filmmaker, Photographer on the not often spoken about’ topics that the community actually needs to attain longevity and a certain level of professionalism.

Brand Strategists, Art Lawyers, Art Curators, Financial Advisors, internationally uccessful Photographers and Filmmakers that have built a good reputation for themselves on international platforms with international brands - all from Africa are set to be the facilitators and resource persons for this workshop.
