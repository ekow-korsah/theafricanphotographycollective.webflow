ABOUT

The African Photography Collective

We are a community of Filmmakers and Photographers, set up to harness the power of unity and partnership with the goal of bridging the gap between the everyday African photographer/filmmaker and the needed resources to enable them attain world class heights and recognition.

We believe that there is enough space globally, for the African to thrive, leave a mark and make Africa proud.

Collaborations and partnerships, mentorship, network sharing, with a sense of oneness and a ‘ no one wins alone ‘ mentality as it’s foundation, The African Photography Collective makes sure that the successful creative, hands down the right map with accurate coordinates to the new and budding ones for the journey now and ahead.

“The Idea is not to live forever; but to create something that will.” - Andy Warhol.
